Shipping and Returns Policy

At Focarium, we strive to process and ship your orders as quickly as possible. Please review our policies below.

Shipping

We offer various shipping options to meet your needs. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and the shipping method selected. We aim to dispatch all orders within 2-3 business days. Please note that delivery times may vary depending on your location.

Returns

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to return or exchange your items. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. Custom or personalized items are not eligible for returns unless they arrive damaged or defective.

Return Process

To initiate a return, please contact us at [Your Email] with your order number and reason for return. We will provide you with return instructions. If the return is due to a defect or error on our part, we will cover the return shipping costs. Otherwise, the customer is responsible for return shipping.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item, please follow the return process and indicate that you would like an exchange. We will process your exchange as soon as we receive the returned item.

If you have any questions about our shipping and returns policy, please contact us at [Your Email].
